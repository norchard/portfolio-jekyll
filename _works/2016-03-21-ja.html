Branding

The Junior Academy logomark joins the traditional collegiate crest with a modern electric blue color palette, appealing to technology-savvy college-bound students.

Information Architecture and Visual Design

The Junior Academy website is the digital campus for remote academic studies. It was designed to educate and entice prospective students and mentors.
